Developing a Resume Polisher

Generative AI

This app will help you fine-tune your resume. You can provide the position you're interested in, the content of your resume, and any specific instructions or areas you'd like the app to focus on (optional). It'll then provide you with a polished version of your content.

In this project I used an Open Source LLM model from Hugingface
