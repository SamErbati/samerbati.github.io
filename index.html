Who I am

A solution designer | data scientist | AI/ML engineer

I'm Sam Erbati, a researcher with a passion for continuous learning and expertise in solution design, data science, artificial intelligence (AI), and machine learning (ML).
I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Duisburg-Essen in Germany, where I focused on expanding knowledge in my field. My academic journey, combined with my professional experiences, has given me a strong understanding of cutting-edge technologies and how to apply them to solve complex problems.

Data Science
